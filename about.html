About Me

Who?

I'm a designer and developer who loves the web. I also have a great interest in 3D and graphic design.

What?

I work on developing front-end UIs and making websites for all sorts of people. I love designing as well so I'm happy to talk about and tackle any project, so just get in touch and let me know what you're working on.

Where?

I'm originally from England, but I lived in France for 10 years and am bilingual. I currently live in Sweden.

Why?

I've always been extremely curious, and since the age of 10 I've been working on websites and figuring out how they work. I genuinely enjoy solving problems and think there's nothing better then when you figure out the perfect way to code something.
